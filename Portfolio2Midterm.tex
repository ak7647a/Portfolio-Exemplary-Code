\section{Introduction}\label{introduction}

This report looks at how much urban and peri‑urban Malawians trust the
police. The data come from Afrobarometer Round 10 (2024) using a
question called Q37G, which asks people, ``How much do you trust the
police?'' Only real answers are included with no missing or ``don't
know'' responses. The goal is to describe overall trust levels and to
see whether men and women in these areas think differently about the
police. All numbers come from the summary tables in the Malawi dataset.
